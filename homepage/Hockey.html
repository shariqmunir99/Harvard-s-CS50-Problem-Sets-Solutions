Sohail The Giant


Sohail Abbas, is a former professional field hockey player from Pakistan who played as a Full-back. Regarded as one of the greatest players of all time he is the highest goal scorer in international field hockey with 348 international goals.He was born in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan, on May 12, 1968. Besides the Olympic Games, he won gold in every other world hockey event. He initially bagged gold medals at one Asia Cup (1989 New Delhi) and one Asian Games (1990 Beijing).
